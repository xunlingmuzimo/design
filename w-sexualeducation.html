A Proficient driver road test/
A Sexual Knowledge test
Model building & Game design   2018

This board game is a relaxed and playful way to talk about a heavy topic and more acceptable to the younger generation. In this game, I’m using the internet term “a proficient driver” in China to indicate a person who has expert sex education knowledge. I want to use this familiar language to approach the younger generation and invite them to talk about the taboo of sex out loud.
