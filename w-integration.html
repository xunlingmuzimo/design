Integration: Minorities in Modern China
Graphic design & Book making

This book was an assignment of Communication studio 4 in Otis. At the beginning, I wrote my manifesto as a young designer and also a Chinese minority to advocate people, especially young generation from China, paying more attention to the intangible heritage of Chinese minorities’ cultures. And at last, I made this book with 3 chapters to introduce the histories and cultures of Chinese minorities, and to tell people what’s happening with those cultures right now.

From Chinese current situation, modernization has a huge impact to the traditional cultures. Chinese modernization took only thirty years while most countries took hundreds of years. Humanity hasn’t ever experience such sudden change, where such a large number of people are going through modernization at such a fast pace. Therefore, protecting intangible cultural heritage will be a crucial task as individuals are more and more ignore their own national cultures.
