Instagram:
Truth or Lie?
Typograhy & Image making   2017

In 2017 summer we had a workshop with Omnivore. During the workshop, we designed three posters based on a discourse about what is fact and lie. My statement was "A top Instagram influencer can charge $25,000 for one post". This statement is entirely the truth, but it can astonish people when they first see it. So I designed those posters to show people the exaggerating distinction between what we thought and what is happening.
