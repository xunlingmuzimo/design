Wind Album
Graphic design & Photography

When I listened to the song from my client Melody, I have had already known what kind of the cover that I want to make. WIND is a gentle but not rigid album, just exactly like Melody, who was young and mature. I took photos form her and modified them with photoshop, after that I got an amazing outcome for the cover. For the color picking, I chose bright blue and monochrome representing a positive and unique style. I designed the whole branding for the album, and also the website.
