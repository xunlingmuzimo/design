WIND Album
Branding & Photography   2015

When I listened to the song from Melody, I have had already known what the cover that I wanted to make was. WIND is an album which is gentle but not rigid, just precisely like the person — Melody, who was young but very smart. I took photos form her and modified them, after that, I got a fantastic outcome for the cover. For the color picking, I chose bright blue and monochrome representing a confident and unique style. In the end, I designed the whole branding for the album, and also the website.
