The Second Nature
Book making & Typography

This book is an anthology of Chinese ethnic minority authors' novels. It involved nine stories and one poem collection inspired by different ethnic minorities' lives and written or edited by ten authors (mostly are ethnic minorities). China is a multi-national country. It has 55 ethnic minorities which constituted about 8.5 percent of the population in China. Almost the main population of ethnic minorities are living in poverty. Over the past 30 years, a variety of social, economic and political forces have pushed them toward assimilation into mainstream Chinese culture. More and more ethnic minorities' young generations are not familiar with their culture......
