Gress Up
Book making & Photography   2016

I shot an interesting series of photos and I decided to use them as my materials of this project. It tells a story about a twelve-years-old girl who try to dress up prettily for her first love. I used a lot of mixed elements of childishness and maturity, because every girls’ first dress-upped date always has some little trouble with outfit. I miss the time that I was that little girl who trying so hard to get one person’s favor. That’s young and beautiful. Based on this theme, I made three posters and a book. I employed bold colors to make the type area vibrant, which also shows the willingness of young girl toward innocent but pure love.
