Baby Works
UI & UX

This is a app concept for helping children building a foundamation of dawing and making 3D products. Children can ‘draw out’ their favorite food with diverse patterns by using the food-ingredients brushes on the digital platform. Their parents will be able to 3D print out the foods by requesting order from Baby Works company. This app provide a great chance for children developing art thinking and craft making ability.
