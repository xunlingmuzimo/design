Neon and girl
Book making & Photography   2016

This book is a magazine about homosexuality among girls who are living in the city. I was always fascinated with the urban planning and the way people interact with the stores, plans, and signs to the town. How the culture, taboo grew up and how the community builds up and influences community members’ perceptions of norms is what the magazine talks about. Neon was the favorite icon of Hongkong, and it stands for trendy, beautiful things. When girls want to be the neon (neo-), how will they decide to break the norms or stay doing nothing?
